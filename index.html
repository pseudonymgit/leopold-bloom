The Process

Simple, transparent, and built around you.

01

The Initial Conversation

We start with a free, no-obligation meeting. We'll discuss your current financial situation, your pain points, and your goals. No preparation needed.

02

Financial Assessment

I'll review your past returns and current books to identify immediate tax savings, compliance issues, and opportunities to streamline your processes.

03

Custom Action Plan

You'll receive a clear, plain-English proposal outlining exactly what we'll do together, how much it will cost, and the timeline for execution.

04

Steady Partnership

Once we begin, you have a dedicated partner. We handle the numbers quietly in the background and meet regularly to ensure you're on track.
